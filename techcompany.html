// Who we are

BUILT BY HACKERS. LED BY EDUCATORS.

CipherSec Academy was founded in 2018 by Meksh Thakkar cybersecurity practitioners who saw a massive gap: India had thousands of students wanting to break into cybersecurity, but almost no training that matched real-world industry demands.

We built what we wished existed — practitioner-led training with live labs, real attack simulations, and a placement engine that actually delivers. Six years later, we've trained 12,400+ students and secured 350+ enterprises. We're not just a training company. We are an active security firm — every instructor is a working professional.
